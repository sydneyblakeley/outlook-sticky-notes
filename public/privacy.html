Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Sticky Notes for Outlook ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What data we collect

  • Microsoft account information — your name and email address, used to identify your account
  • Meeting notes — the text you type into your sticky notes, stored securely and associated with your account
  • Action items — action items you add manually or that are imported from connected notetaker services
  • Meeting metadata — meeting titles and dates, used to associate your notes with the correct calendar event

What we do NOT collect

  • We never read or store the body or content of your calendar invites
  • We never read your email messages
  • We never share your notes or data with other meeting attendees
  • We never sell your data to third parties

How we use your data

  • To display your notes when you open a calendar event
  • To sync your notes across devices
  • To provide the action item and meeting recap features
  • To manage your subscription status

Third party services

If you connect a notetaker integration (such as Fireflies.ai or Otter.ai), we retrieve your action items from that service using your API credentials. We do not store your notetaker credentials — they are stored securely as environment variables and never exposed.

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your payment card details.

Data storage

Your notes and account data are stored in a secured database hosted on Supabase (AWS infrastructure). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you have an active account. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

Your rights

  • Access your data at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your notes

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: support@stickynotes.app
