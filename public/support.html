Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install Sticky Notes for Outlook?

Search for "Sticky Notes for Outlook" in the Microsoft AppSource marketplace or in the Get Add-ins section within Outlook. Click Install and the add-in will appear in your calendar events automatically.

Will other meeting attendees see my notes?

No — your sticky notes are completely private. They are never shared with other attendees or included in the meeting invite.

How do I connect Fireflies.ai?

A Fireflies.ai account is required for the Pro + Notetaker plan. Once your meeting is recorded and processed by Fireflies, your action items will automatically appear in the Actions tab when you open that meeting in Outlook.

Will my notes sync across devices?

Your notes are stored locally in your browser. To access them on another device, use the same browser and Outlook account.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime by contacting us at stickynotes.help@outlook.com. Your notes will remain accessible until the end of your billing period.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email us at stickynotes.help@outlook.com and we will delete your account and all associated data within 5 business days.

My action items aren't importing from Fireflies

Confirm the meeting was recorded and fully processed by Fireflies before opening it in Outlook. Meeting titles must match closely for the import to work. If issues persist, contact support.
